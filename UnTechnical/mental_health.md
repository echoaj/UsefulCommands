# Hidden Brain
[Befrending Your Inner Voice](https://hiddenbrain.org/podcast/you-2-0-befriending-your-inner-voice/) is a podcast that explores the unconscious patterns that drive human behavior, relationships, and decision-making.
## Tips
* Talk to yourself using your name and "you" instead of "I" and "me".
* View your circumstances not as a threat but as a challenge.
* Whatever we are dealing with, it will eventually pass.
## Notes
* In cultures around the world, they use rituals and prayer to decrease stress and anxiety.  It's a way to feel control and distract.
* You see the same thing in sports.  Athletes use rituals to get into the zone.

# Stress Management
## Tips
* Get enough sleep. Diet and exercise. Take breaks. Breath
* Make time to realx and unwind.
* Connect with friends and family.
* Don't stop doing things you enjoy.
## Notes
* Stress triggers the fight or flight response.  It's a survival mechanism.
* In the old days we would encounter tigers but those tigers are now replaced by deadlines, work, and bills.  But our body still reacts the same way.
* Burnout is a real thing.  It's a state of physical, emotional, and mental exhaustion caused by excessive and prolonged stress.

# Communication
## Tips
* Listen to understand, not to respond.
* Body language is important.  Don't cross your arms.  Don't look away.
* Think before you speak
## Notes
* As digital consumption goes up, the level of empathy goes down.
* We are more impatient.
* We are constantly interupted.
* Just because we have more communication chanels doesn't mean our communication quality is better.
* We need to enchance our focus on analgog communication skills rather than digital communication skills.
* Within 3 seconds of meeting someone, we have already made a judgement about them.
